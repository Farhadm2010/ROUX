# ROUX 🍴

Your AI culinary companion — mastering recipes, solving kitchen problems, and teaching techniques from the world's greatest cookbooks.

## What is ROUX?

ROUX is a custom GPT powered by a database of 25,000+ recipes, 9,700+ culinary information entries, and 5,600+ troubleshooting guides sourced from professional cookbooks across French, Italian, Japanese, and global cuisines.

## What ROUX Can Do

- 🍳 Find recipes by ingredient, cuisine, technique, or dish name
- 🔧 Troubleshoot cooking problems (why did my sauce break? why is my bread dense?)
- 📚 Explain culinary techniques and terminology
- 🌍 Search across multiple languages and cuisines
- 💡 Suggest substitutions and variations

## Knowledge Base

| Category | Entries |
|----------|---------|
| Recipes | 10,333 |
| Informational | 9,780 |

Sourced from professional culinary books covering French, Italian, Japanese, pastry, bread, meat, seafood, and more.

## Tech Stack

- **Platform:** ChatGPT Custom GPT
- **Database:** Supabase (PostgreSQL)
- **Search:** Real-time API calls with semantic matching
- **Languages supported:** English, French, Italian, Japanese
